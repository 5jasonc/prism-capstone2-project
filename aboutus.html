About Us

How can we spark hope?

The past year has been a period of distress and isolation, leaving many of us feeling alone and discouraged.

Introducing wishful

Wishful allows anyone to explore a virtual world, populated by the hopes and wishes of others, cultivating a community to bring each other up by sharing our dreams for the future.

Why Jellyfish?

Jellyfish have a symbolic connection to personal reflection, by making a wish you are giving life to a moment of vulnerability.

Bringing hope to the community

With an entirely virtual experience, we thought of ways of bringing inspiration and joy to the physical world.

Taking the experience to a new dimension

Using augmented reality, watch as your jellyfish moves into the world in front of you.

About Us

A year of hopelessnes

The past year has been a period of distress and isolation, leaving many of us feeling alone and discouraged.

How do we spark hope?

We are creating an enchanting, exploratory experience to allow people to express their hope for the future and find comradery with others.

A digital wishing well

The process involves transforming your personal wish into a jellyfish-like virtual creature, swimming amongst the hopes and wishes of others, and culminating a community of these personal wishes that can be shared and saved.

Why Jellyfish?

Jellyfish have a symbolic connection to personal reflection, by making a wish you are giving life to a moment of vulnerability.
